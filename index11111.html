Health is Wealth app, would help a user in setting up a health routine on a daily basis based on the time they get up. The user is prompted to enter their waking up time. Based on this, the application would by default, unless changed would prompt user to get a glass of water after every 2 hours, prompting user to have a minimal 8 glasses of water per day. Similarly, the application would remind user to go for a small walk after some default set of time unless changed. Another thing that the application does, would be considering the user would wake up the next day around the same time, suggesting him to get 8 hour sleep, by 'go to bed' reminders.

There are apps that take care of the above individually. The major advantage here would be, the application will make users' schedule more disciplined.





WeShareFood<3 is an application, where you could connect to your friends and colleagues. The application aims at sharing user's recent visits to any restaurants and their respective rating for that particular restaurant. Users can mark their favourite cuisines in their profile. Any time a friend of your's goes to a restaurant of user's cuisine, user will be notified about their experience.

Zomato a similar application, lacks in the sense that you have to go a particular restaurant to check for it's ratings. In my application, users recent visit and their ratings for the respective restaurant visit will be seen in your updates page.
